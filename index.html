Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse

This movie follows a teenage boy, Miles Morales, as he gets stumbles into the happenings of the previous Spider-Man and gets bit by a radio active spider. Through the movie multipls different Spider-mans appear, and he has to find a way to bring them back to their respective universes. It is a world-recognized film for its ground-breaking animation.
